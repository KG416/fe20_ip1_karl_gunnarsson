Traditional food with a spice of progressive habanero!

Our restaurant probably has the finest wall paper you will ever see. They're really old! Once we had a guest saying the walls made him question his career (he was an carpenter). He honestly sat by the fireplace next to our bar and went on and on about how working full time as a wall felt more in the lines of how he saw himself on the inside (he touched his chest right where he presumed the heart was and looked deep into a list of emergency phone numbers). "What had happened?", he said. "When I was that little kid running around in Vladvostock (yes, he travelled far) I never dreamed about nails, glue or hammers!" Sure enough that guy came around the day after, kept his job and so forth. The point is though, you now have a clear picture of what to expect when eating at our restaurant. We look forward to your visit!
